Grand Canyon National Park

Featuring several breathtaking views looking over the Colorado River, the Grand Canyon reveals millions of years of geologic history and offers endless hiking opportunities, making it a popular tourist destination.


Location: Arizona


Ideal Seasons to Visit:

  • March to May
  • September to November


Popular Activites:

  • Walking and Hiking
  • Mule trips
  • Raft trips


Safety:

  • Stay on designated trails and walkways
  • Always keep a safe distance of at least six feet from the edge of the rim
  • Never throw rocks, coins, trash or anything else over the edge
  • Stay hydrated by drinking lots of water
