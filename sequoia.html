Sequoia National Park

Home to the largest towering sequoia trees in the world, Sequoia National Park offers stunning hiking paths that allow visitors to admire the trees, rivers, meadows, and wildlife of California.


Location: California


Ideal Seasons to Visit:

  • June to August


Popular Activites:

  • Visit the Giant Forest
  • Hiking
  • Visit the Crystal Cave


Safety:

  • Bring hats, sunscreen, and light colored clothing to protect you from the sun
  • Be aware of poison ivy
  • Properly store all food, scented items, and food related supplies
  • Take extreme caution when crossing streams, especially if the water is high and swift
  • For other recommended preparations, view the Ten Essentials checklist provided by the National Park Service.


Opening Hours:

  • Sequoia National Park is open 24 hours all year round. However, most people visit during the summer since some park roads are closed during the winter months.
