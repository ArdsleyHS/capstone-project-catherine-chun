Zion National Park

Distinguished by steep red cliffs and forest trails along the Virgin River, Zion Natural Park protects several archaeological sites and offers scenic hikes and drives through rivers, canyons, and waterfalls.


Location: Utah


Ideal Seasons to Visit:

  • April to May
  • September to October


Popular Activites:

  • Camping
  • Rockclimbing
  • Riverside trail


Safety:

  • Stay on designated trails
  • Stay back from cliff edges
  • Stay hydrated by drinking lots of water
  • Pay close attention to your surroundings, specifically be aware of rockfalls
