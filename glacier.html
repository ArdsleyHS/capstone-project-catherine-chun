Glacier National Park

Spanning over two mountain ranges and including more than seven hundred lakes of northern Montana, Glacier National Park is perfect for hikers seeking adventure, wilderness, and solitude.


Location: Montana


Ideal Seasons to Visit:

  • July
  • August


Popular Activites:

  • Hiking
  • Backcountry camping
  • Fishing


Safety:

  • Keep a safe distance from bears, wolves, and other wildlife
  • Hike in groups to decrease your chances of a bear encounter
  • Carry bear spray
  • Secure your food and garbage
  • Always be aware of your surroundings


Opening Hours:

  • Glacier National Park is open 24 hours all year round. However, most visitors visit during the summer months because most services in and near the park are closed all winter.
