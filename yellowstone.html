Yellowstone National Park

Best known for its luscious waterfalls, deep valleys, and grand meadows, a visit to Yosemite allows you to take in the breathtaking wonders of the High Sierra.


Location: Sierra Nevada Mountains, California


Ideal Seasons to Visit:

  • April to May
  • September to October


Popular Activites:

  • Watch geysers erupt
  • Hiking
  • Go rafting or padling


Safety:

  • Never approach wildlife
  • Never feed wildife
  • Stay on boardwalks and trails in thermal areas
  • Never park in the road or block traffic
