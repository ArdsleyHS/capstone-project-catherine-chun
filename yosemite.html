Yosemite National Park

Featuring dramatic canyons, alpine rivers, lush forests, and hot springs, Yellowstone makes for a beautiful experience of nature, wildlife, and geologic wonders.


Location: Wyoming, parts of Montana and Idaho


Ideal Seasons to Visit:

  • May
  • September


Popular Activites:

  • Camping
  • Rock climbing
  • Hiking


Safety:

  • Maintain your distance from animals even if they approach you
  • Always practice proper food storage
  • Keep bicycles on paved roads or trails
  • Never swim or wade upstream from the brink of a waterfall
  • Be aware of changing conditions and have a plan in case a thunderstorm approaches


Opening Hours:

  • Yosemite National Park is open 24 hours all year round. However, for over night hikes and camping, you need to apply for a wildnerness permit and make camping reservations.
